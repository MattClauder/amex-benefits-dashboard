💰 Walmart+ / Peacock / Paramount+ Stack

STACK Walmart+ is FREE via your Platinum Card ($12.95/mo credit). Inside Walmart+, you get Peacock Premium OR Paramount+ (ad-supported) completely free. That's a hidden ~$80/year streaming benefit on top of the Walmart+ value. Go to your Walmart+ Benefits Hub to activate.
ACTION You can switch between Peacock and Paramount+ every 90 days. Rotate based on what shows/movies you want to watch each quarter. Set a calendar reminder!
WARNING Cancel your existing Peacock/Paramount+ subscription FIRST. The free Walmart+ streaming benefit won't activate if you have an active paid subscription. Let it expire, then activate through Walmart+.
MONEY Don't forget other Walmart+ perks: Free delivery on groceries ($35+ orders), fuel savings (up to 10¢/gal at Exxon, Mobil, Walmart & Murphy stations), free shipping with no order minimum, mobile scan & go, and Paramount+/Peacock streaming.

🧠 Platinum Card Pro Tips

WARNING Subscribe to streaming services DIRECTLY through their website (e.g., disneyplus.com, hulu.com). Subscriptions paid via Apple App Store or Amazon won't trigger the $25/mo Digital Entertainment credit.
MONEY December Uber Cash bonus: You get $20 in December instead of $15. Plan a nice Uber Eats dinner or rides to holiday events!
STACK Uber One + Uber Cash stack: Pay for Uber One ($120/yr credit) with your Platinum, then use your monthly $15 Uber Cash on top. Uber One gives $0 delivery fees and 5% off rides/deliveries. Double dipping!
MONEY Airline Credit hack: Select United as your airline and load funds into United TravelBank. As of early 2026, this still triggers the $200 airline fee credit. You can then use TravelBank funds for future United flights.
STACK Delta strategy: Use Delta eCredits or SkyMiles (Pay with Miles) to cover part of a ticket, then charge the remainder ($250 or less) to your Platinum to trigger the airline fee credit.
ACTION Saks credit tip: Buy a small item or gift card at Saks to use the $50 semi-annual credit if you don't regularly shop there. Don't let it expire.
WARNING ENROLL IN EVERY BENEFIT IMMEDIATELY. Most credits require enrollment before purchases will trigger statement credits. Go to your Amex account → Benefits → Enroll in each one. If you don't enroll, you get nothing.

🥇 Gold Card Pro Tips

MONEY Use Grubhub for pickup orders to get the $10/mo dining credit without paying delivery fees. Or order from The Cheesecake Factory / Five Guys in person.
STACK Gold + Platinum Uber Cash stack: Link BOTH cards to the same Uber account. You'll get $15/mo from Platinum + $10/mo from Gold = $25/month in Uber Cash!
MONEY 4x MR points at restaurants and U.S. supermarkets (up to $25K/yr at supermarkets). This is on top of the dining credits. Use Gold for all dining/grocery spending.
ACTION Dunkin' credit tip: Reload your Dunkin' app balance using your Gold Card to trigger the $7/mo credit. Or just pay at the register with your card.
STACK Resy stacking: Both your Gold ($100/yr) and Platinum ($400/yr) have separate Resy credits. That's $500/year in Resy dining combined. Use different cards at different Resy restaurants each quarter.

🏢 Business Platinum Pro Tips

STACK Dell credit + shopping portal: Buy through a shopping portal (like Rakuten) to earn extra cash back on top of your Dell credit and Amex points. Triple dip!
ACTION Dell sells more than computers: Use the $150 Dell credit on monitors, keyboards, mice, headsets, webcams, printers, or even home electronics. Check Dell.com/amex for deals.
ACTION Indeed credit ($360/yr): Even if you're not actively hiring, you can use Indeed credits for job postings, resume searches, or employer branding. Don't let $90/quarter go to waste.
MONEY Hilton $200/yr credit: Use the $50/quarter for Hilton stays. This is separate from the FHR/Hotel Collection credit — you can use BOTH for different trips!
ACTION Wireless $120/yr: Just put your cell phone bill on the Business Platinum. $10/month automatically credits back. Works with Verizon, AT&T, T-Mobile, etc.
MONEY Adobe $250 credit: If you have Adobe Creative Cloud ($55/mo), you'll hit the $600 threshold in about 11 months and get $250 back. Effectively cuts your Adobe cost nearly in half.

🔥 Cross-Card Mega Tips

STACK Double CLEAR+ credit: Both your Platinum ($209) and Business Platinum ($209) offer CLEAR+ credits. Use one for yourself and the other for a family member's CLEAR+ membership!
STACK Double Global Entry: Same idea — use both Platinum and Business Platinum Global Entry credits for two family members.
STACK $1,200/year in hotel credits: Both your Platinum and Business Platinum offer $600/year in FHR/Hotel Collection credits. That's $1,200 combined for luxury hotel stays!
MONEY Free streaming empire: With your Platinum's $25/mo Digital Entertainment credit + Walmart+'s free Peacock/Paramount+, you can have YouTube TV ($73/mo partially covered), Disney+ Bundle, NYT, WSJ, AND Peacock/Paramount+ with most costs offset.
